Dhanashree online sellers offers free shipping within India on all products. We pay for shipping, octroi and other taxes. You only pay the value of the ordered product. If for any reason, you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it for up to 30 days from the original purchase date for a refund or credit note. In order to do so the item(s) must be in new, unworn condition with all packaging and garment tags still attached.
